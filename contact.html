FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Clear answers for clients, families and referrers

Who do you support?

We support older adults, individuals with disabilities and families seeking dependable home and community-based support.

Do you only cover Manchester?

No. Our coverage includes Greater Manchester and Cheshire.

Can support be tailored to routines and preferences?

Yes. Our approach is person-centred, so services are planned around the individual rather than a standard model.

Can professionals refer clients?

Yes. We welcome professional enquiries and referrals as well as private family enquiries.
